Software Dispute Defense in High stakes Litigation 
March 15, 2025

Our client, an emerging technology company, was sued by a large, established competitor for trade secret misappropriation and tortious interference. The plaintiff filed for a preliminary injunction to stop our client's product launch, a move that would have been fatal to the company. Our team responded immediately, successfully defeating the injunction by demonstrating that the alleged "trade secrets" were, in fact, public information. This aggressive early victory forced the plaintiff to the negotiating table, where they agreed to a "walk-away" settlement with no payment from our client.
