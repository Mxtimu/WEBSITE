FAQ

What types of cases do you handle?

We handle a wide range of legal matters, including civil, criminal, and corporate cases. Our team can assist with everything from contract disputes to criminal defense and family law. If you're unsure whether your issue falls within our services, we offer consultations to point you in the right direction.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office, submitting the online contact form, or emailing us directly. We offer both in-person and virtual meeting options for your convenience.

How do I know if I have a strong case?

The best way to know is to speak with an attorney. During your consultation, we’ll review the facts, explain your options, and help you understand your best legal path.
